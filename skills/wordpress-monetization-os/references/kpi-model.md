# KPI Model (Monetization OS)

Primary KPIs
- Revenue per 1,000 sessions (RPS)
- Affiliate EPC
- Affiliate CTR
- Conversion rate (offer click -> sale/lead)
- Opt-in rate

Secondary KPIs
- Bounce delta after monetization changes
- Scroll depth on money pages
- CWV impact after ad/offer injection

Priority scoring
Expected Lift Score = (Traffic Score * Intent Score * Gap Score * Confidence) - Risk Penalty

Execute highest score first.
