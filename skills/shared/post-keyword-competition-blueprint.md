# Post-Keyword Competition Blueprint

Use this after choosing a target keyword and before drafting the page.

## Goal
Turn a promising keyword into a competitive blueprint that is actually rankable.

## Workflow
1. Search the keyword
2. Keep only competitor pages clearly targeting the keyword in:
   - title
   - URL
3. Ignore incidental rankings that are not strongly aligned
4. Extract the subtopics/secondary keywords those filtered competitors cover
5. Keep only the terms where multiple top competitors overlap
6. Treat that overlap set as the minimum content blueprint for your page
7. Identify domains linking to several of those competitor pages
8. Prioritize those backlink targets because they already link to similar content

## Required Output
- target keyword
- filtered competitor URLs
- overlap subtopics/content requirements
- internal-link opportunities
- backlink overlap targets

## Principle
Strong keyword research is not just finding demand.
It is turning SERP overlap and backlink overlap into a page blueprint that can actually compete.
