# Money Page Optimization Standard

Use this for buyer-intent pages where rankings and monetization matter most.

## Applies to
- Best/roundup pages
- Comparison pages
- Review pages
- Commercial investigation pages

## Required elements
1. Clear buyer-intent title/H1
2. Strong intro that resolves intent fast
3. One obvious primary recommendation path or decision framework
4. Internal links to:
   - 1 pillar/tool page
   - 2-3 related articles
   - 1 closely related money page
5. Trust layer:
   - affiliate disclosure available
   - review methodology available
   - editorial/about policy available
6. Comparison clarity:
   - tables/cards easy to scan
   - winner/use-case framing where appropriate
7. Mobile safety:
   - cards/tables/buttons stay in bounds
   - screenshots required if visual changes were made

## Money-page content blueprint
- what this page is for
- who it is best for
- how to decide fast
- product/page comparisons
- decision shortcuts
- next-step links

## Anti-patterns
- generic fitness intros on commercial running pages
- weak internal linking to no-conversion endpoints
- no trust/disclosure support
- visual breakage on mobile
- adding affiliate links without decision support
