# On-Page Integration Rules (Enterprise)

## Placement
- Primary keyword: title + H1 + one early-body mention.
- Secondary keywords: section headers/body where semantically natural.
- Entities: spread across intro, methods, comparison/FAQ blocks.

## Language quality
- Avoid awkward exact-match repeats.
- Prefer contextual synonyms and related concepts.
- Keep user-first clarity over density targets.

## Anti-stuffing heuristics
- Do not repeat exact phrase in consecutive headers.
- Avoid repetitive sentence templates with swapped keywords.
- Remove forced insertions that reduce readability.

## Trust standards
- No clickbait/fearbait patterns.
- No unsupported absolutes ("best ever", "guaranteed").
- Keep claims specific and evidence-ready.

## Internal linking
- Use intent-aligned anchor text.
- Link to true adjacent subtopics, not random keyword matches.
