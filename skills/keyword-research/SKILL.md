# Keyword Research

## Objective
Build a prioritized keyword map that is publish-ready (not just a long list).

## Scope Boundaries
- Include: discovery, intent classification, difficulty estimation, clustering, prioritization.
- Exclude: full article drafting (use seo-content-writer), deep technical audits, backlink campaigns.

## Workflow
1. **Confirm inputs**: site/domain, niche, geo/language, business goals, conversion actions.
2. **Generate seed set**: products/services, audience problems, modifiers, entity variants.
3. **Expand universe**: long-tail, question, comparison, and transactional permutations.
4. **Classify intent**: informational, commercial investigation, transactional, navigational.
5. **Score opportunity** per keyword:
   - Demand (volume/trend)
   - Difficulty/competition
   - Relevance to offer
   - SERP fit (can we realistically win?)
6. **Cluster keywords** into pages/topics to prevent cannibalization.
7. **Prioritize execution**: quick wins, strategic pillars, and supporting articles.
8. **Map to content types**: landing page, product page, blog post, comparison, FAQ.

## Quality Gates
- No keyword list without intent labels.
- No priority list without rationale.
- No cluster plan that creates obvious cannibalization.
- Prefer realistic opportunities over vanity head terms.

## Output Artifacts
Return:
1. Ranked keyword table (keyword, intent, difficulty proxy, priority, target URL/content type).
2. Cluster map (primary + supporting terms).
3. 30/60/90-day execution order.
4. Assumptions + data gaps needing validation.

## Handoff Rules
- If user asks for briefs/outlines, hand off to seo-content-brief.
- If user asks for full drafts, hand off to seo-content-writer.
