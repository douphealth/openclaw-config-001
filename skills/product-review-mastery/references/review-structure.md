# Review Structure (Canonical)

## A. Hook (100-150 words)
- Mirror reader decision context
- Clarify why this review exists
- Set scope and what is tested

## B. Answer-First Verdict (40-70 words)
- Direct recommendation + buyer-fit qualifier

## C. Quick Snapshot
- Score, price range, core strengths, key weakness

## D. Buyer Fit
- Best for / Not ideal for / Consider alternatives if...

## E. Testing Methodology
- How product was evaluated, limits of testing

## F. Deep-Dive Sections
- Performance
- Ease of use
- Reliability/support
- Price-to-value

## G. Pros & Cons
- Balanced and specific

## H. Alternatives
- At least one practical alternative with clear “why”

## I. Final Verdict + Confidence Level
- high / moderate / qualified confidence

## J. FAQ
- 6-10 questions, unique answers

## K. Trust Footer
- Editorial process, disclosure, update date
