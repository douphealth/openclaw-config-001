# Portfolio Paid Diagnosis

## Before blaming media
Check whether the target site actually has a trustworthy money path.

### efficientgptprompts.com
Paid diagnosis is invalid if checkout, payment visibility, or delivery/access are unproven.

### affiliatemarketingforsuccess.com
Paid diagnosis is incomplete if signup works but deterministic first-email automation does not.

### gearuptogrow.com
Paid diagnosis is weak if the service offer and CTA path are still under-positioned or unclear.

## Rule
A weak destination can make competent paid media look broken.
