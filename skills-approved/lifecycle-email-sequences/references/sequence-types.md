# Sequence Types

## Welcome
Goal: orient, build trust, get first action.

## Nurture
Goal: educate, deepen belief, move toward conversion.

## Onboarding
Goal: activation and aha moment.

## Re-engagement
Goal: revive interest or clean the list.

## Post-purchase
Goal: retention, expansion, referral, or successful use.

## Default timing guidance
- immediate for first welcome / delivery email
- 1-2 days for early steps
- 2-4 days for nurture spacing
- weekly for longer-term sequences when appropriate
