# Headline Formulas for Conversion Copy

Conversion-focused headline templates by page type and audience awareness level.
Unlike SEO titles (≤60 chars), conversion headlines can be longer if they sell.

## Headline Formulas by Page Type

### Homepage (Route to Next Step)
- [Outcome] for [Audience]: [Mechanism] That [Specific Result]
  Example: "Marketing Automation for SaaS: The System That Cut Our CAC by 40%"
- [Number] [Audience] Use [Product] to [Achieve Outcome]
  Example: "4,200 B2B Teams Use CampaignOS to Launch 3x More Campaigns"
- [Audience] Are [Problem]. [Product] Fixes It.
  Example: "E-commerce Brands Are Losing 30% of Abandoned Carts. RecoverThem Fixes It in One Click."

### Landing Page (Single Conversion)
- Get [Specific Outcome] in [Timeframe] — [Mechanism or Proof]
  Example: "Get 2x More Qualified Leads in 30 Days — The Scoring Framework Used by HubSpot, Salesforce, and 1,200+ B2B Teams"
- The [Adjective] Way to [Achieve Outcome] (Without [Common Pain Point])
  Example: "The Smartest Way to Segment Your Email List (Without Hiring a Data Analyst)"
- [Number] [Positive Outcome] in [Timeframe]. Here's Exactly How.
  Example: "743 Trial-to-Paid Conversions in 90 Days. Here's Exactly How We Did It."

### Pricing Page (Choose a Plan)
- [Outcome] at Every Level — Start Free, Scale When Ready
  Example: "Double Your Email Revenue at Every Level — Start Free, Scale When Ready"
- The Only [Category] You'll Ever Need — [Tier Differentiation]
  Example: "The Only Email Platform You'll Ever Need — From Startup to Enterprise"
- Pay for [Outcome], Not [Pain Point]
  Example: "Pay for Conversions, Not Clicks"

### Product/Feature Page (Understand Value)
- [Outcome] Without [Pain Point]
  Example: "Full Campaign Attribution Without Hiring a Data Team"
- How [Audience] [Achieve Outcome] with [Feature]
  Example: "How SaaS Teams Cut Setup Time from 3 Weeks to 5 Minutes with One-Click Integrations"
- [Feature]: [Outcome] on Autopilot
  Example: "Smart Segmentation: Always-Updated Lists on Autopilot"

### Offer/CTA (Urgency + Specificity)
- [Time-Limited] + [Benefit] + [Social Proof]
  Example: "This Week Only: 40% Off Annual Plans — Join 4,200+ Teams Already Using It"
- [Outcome] — Before [Deadline/Scarcity]
  Example: "Lock In Founder Pricing — Before We Raise Rates on March 31"
- Free [Valuable Resource] — [Specificity]
  Example: "Free CRO Audit Template — The Same One We Use for $10K/Month Clients"

## Headline Quality Checklist

Rate each headline 1-5 on each dimension:

| Dimension | Score 5 | Score 3 | Score 1 |
|-----------|---------|---------|---------|
| Specificity | Numbers, names, timeframes | Some specifics | Vague, generic |
| Outcome | Clear benefit for reader | Implied benefit | No benefit stated |
| Believability | Proof element included | Believable claim | Too good to be true |
| Clarity | Instantly understood | Needs re-reading | Confusing |
| Uniqueness | Only fits this product | Could fit 10 products | Could fit 100 products |

**Target:** Average score ≥ 4.0 across all dimensions.

## Headline Testing Protocol

1. Write 3-5 headline variants
2. Score each on the checklist above
3. Test top 2 via A/B split (if traffic allows)
4. Track: CTR, scroll depth, conversion rate
5. Winner becomes control. Test next challenger in 2 weeks.

## Common Headline Mistakes
- ❌ Clever wordplay that confuses ("Revolutionize Your Synergy" — what?)
- ❌ No outcome stated ("Welcome to Our Platform" — so what?)
- ❌ Too long (>2 lines on mobile — gets cut off)
- ❌ Generic: "The Best [Category] for [Year]" — could be anyone
- ❌ No proof: "10x Your Revenue" — with what evidence?
- ❌ Missing audience: great headline but doesn't say WHO it's for
- ❌ Feature-led: "AI-Powered Analytics Dashboard" → flip to benefit: "Know Which Ads Drive Revenue — In One Click"
