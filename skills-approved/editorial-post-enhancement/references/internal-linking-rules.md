# Internal Linking Rules

## Quality bar
- use contextual rich anchor text
- prefer pages that deepen or advance the reader journey
- avoid stuffing many links into one section
- spread links across the article naturally
- verify key targets return 200 when practical

## Good patterns
- supporting explainer page
- adjacent commercial page
- topic-cluster page
- tool/comparison page
- next-step monetization page

## Bad patterns
- random archive links
- vague anchor text like "click here"
- five links in one paragraph
- linking to weak or off-topic pages just to increase count
