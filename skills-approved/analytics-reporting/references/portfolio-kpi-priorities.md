# Portfolio KPI Priorities

## efficientgptprompts.com
Primary KPIs:
- add-to-cart rate
- checkout start rate
- purchase rate
- delivery/access success rate
- consulting upsell or lead capture rate

## affiliatemarketingforsuccess.com
Primary KPIs:
- signup conversion rate
- contact creation success rate
- welcome email send rate
- service CTA progression rate
- qualified lead rate

## gearuptogrow.com
Primary KPIs:
- homepage CTA rate
- service page conversion rate
- inquiry / booking rate
- qualified lead rate

## Rule
Report the KPI closest to money first, then supporting engagement metrics.
