# Reporting Quality Bars

## Minimum viable reporting
Include:
- question being answered
- source and date range
- comparison period
- top 3 findings
- confidence / caveat note
- recommended action

## Decision-grade reporting
Add:
- segmentation that changes the decision
- leading vs lagging indicator distinction
- baseline vs current vs target
- explicit unknowns
- what should happen next and who should care

## Anti-patterns
- chart dumps without interpretation
- totals without trend
- executive summary with no decision
- unexplained attribution jumps
