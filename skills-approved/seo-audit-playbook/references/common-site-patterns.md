# Common Site Patterns

## SaaS
Watch for:
- thin feature pages
- missing comparison pages
- weak product-to-content linking

## Ecommerce
Watch for:
- thin category pages
- duplicate descriptions
- faceted navigation duplicates

## Blog / publisher
Watch for:
- cannibalization
- weak clustering
- stale content

## Local business
Watch for:
- NAP inconsistency
- weak location pages
- poor local trust signals
