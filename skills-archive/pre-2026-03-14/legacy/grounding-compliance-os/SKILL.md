# Grounding Compliance OS

## Scope Ownership
### Own
- Execute Claim-grounding and evidence compliance workflow for SEO/content operations. Use when validating factual, statistical, or performance claims before publishing or reporting outcomes.
- Apply workflows, gates, and outputs defined in this file and its references/scripts.

### Do Not Own
- Do not override responsibilities of more specific domain skills when one clearly matches.
- Do not claim completion for adjacent systems without their direct verification gates.

## Claim safety protocol
1. extract claims from draft/report
2. classify: `factual | numeric | performance | comparative | opinion`
3. require evidence for all non-opinion claims
4. rewrite unsupported numeric/performance claims into defensible wording

## Evidence gate
A claim passes only when source evidence exists and matches meaning.
If uncertain:
- downgrade certainty language
- mark partial/degraded
- never overstate

## Deliverable
- grounded claims list
- unsupported claims list
- rewrites applied
- residual risk notes
