# Sovereign SEO Audit

Execute in order:
1) Crawl + indexability
2) Technical foundations
3) On-page quality
4) Content + intent fit
5) Internal linking + architecture
6) Prioritized remediation plan

## 1) Crawl + indexability
- Validate HTTP status, robots directives, canonicals, sitemap coverage.
- Identify accidental noindex/canonical drift/orphaned critical URLs.

## 2) Technical foundations
- Check CWV signals (LCP/INP/CLS), render blocking, mobile usability, HTTPS hygiene.
- Flag failures by severity (P0/P1/P2).

## 3) On-page quality
- Audit title/H1/meta alignment, heading structure, entity coverage, duplicate signals.
- Detect thin, stale, or keyword-stuffed pages.

## 4) Content + intent fit
- Match page purpose to query intent (informational/commercial/transactional).
- Improve answer-first clarity and decision support.

## 5) Internal linking + architecture
- Evaluate hub/spoke pathways, anchor quality, crawl depth, and orphan risk.
- Recommend authority flow improvements to money/pillar pages.

## 6) Prioritized remediation plan
- Output ordered backlog with owner, effort, risk, impact, and verification steps.
- Use reversible, high-leverage fixes first.

## Required deliverables
- Overall score
- Critical findings (P0/P1)
- Top opportunities
- 7-day execution plan
- Evidence paths

## Guardrails
- No fabricated ranking or traffic claims.
- Do not mark fixed until source + live verification pass.
